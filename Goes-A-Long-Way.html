Just A Little Goes A Long Way



Teenagers create a huge amount of waste materials from the activities they do, that ought to be recycled. We must stop this or else there won't be a small place for us to breathe any longer.

We cannot think that others will find an answer to this problem any longer. Instead of thinking others will find a solution, we must admit our responsibilities and do the small actions that are needed to make this world a better and more wonderful place.


There are lot of advantages we can experience by recycling waste as a teenager. For an examples,


1. You can save and earn money.


2. Reduce the Global Warming.


3. Make the environment around you more wonderful.

The fact is, everyone, needs to get into the habit of using fewer materials, and the things we do use ought to be recycled, to decrease the waste we dispose of. If all of us help to recycle the waste materials that we throw away, no matter if the help is small, we can do a great change.
